Application Overview

T-Link is a smartphone screen sharing application designed for Display Audio.
With convenient 2-way touch control capability.
T-Link maximizes your in-car experience.

[ How to connect with Display Audio ]
Sound sharing: by Bluetooth connection
Screen sharing: by USB cable connection

[ Remarks ]
Can share any smartphone application to Display Audio.
Some operations from the Display Audio side are restricted while driving a car depending to application.
Some functions may do not work by operation on Display Audio depending on the connected device.
